SERVICES

SNOB INC is a full service social media agency, we provide the following services to brands and clients

1 / STRATEGY

We develop a multi-faceted social media strategy that includes creative direction, specific platform goals, and campaign concepts. This cohesive plan will drive brand awareness, grow followers and engagement, and maximize your online presence.

2 / CONTENT CREATION

We develop mood boards, oversee planning, production, and execution of content shoots on a consistent basis. Includes photography, styling, post-production as well as video and editing.

3 / SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

We use social media platforms to connect with your audiences, build your brand, increase sales and leads, and drive website traffic by developing organic and paid content that captures the consumers' interest

4 / PLATFORM MANAGEMENT

We develop social media calendars that include a combination of brand building, promotions, and timely opportunities. We maximize the roll-out of campaigns and release posts at peak times for best visibility.

5 / COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

We develop unique community response language that aligns with your voice and we engage with your audience on a consistent basis.

6 / INFLUENCERS PROGRAMS

We develop a custom database of influencers / creators on behalf of your brand or project, and oversee a successful partnership. We focus on brand alignment in order to maximize awareness and conversion.
